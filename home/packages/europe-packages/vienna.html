3 Days in Vienna

📍 Austria | ⏱ 3 Days

Vienna is a beautiful city known for its classical music, stunning palaces, and rich history. Visitors can explore the elegant Schönbrunn Palace, enjoy coffee in traditional cafés, and stroll through charming streets filled with culture and art.
